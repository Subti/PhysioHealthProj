Understanding and Managing Neck and Back Pain

Introduction

Neck and back pain are common ailments that can affect anyone. Often, these discomforts arise from prolonged periods of sitting, especially in non-ergonomic setups that don't support good posture. With the rise of remote work and increased screen time, it's more important than ever to understand the causes of neck and back pain, and how to manage them.

Causes of Neck and Back Pain

Poor posture and lack of movement are primary causes of neck and back pain. When we sit for extended periods, especially if we're hunched over a computer, it puts strain on our neck and spine. Other factors include:

  • Overuse injuries
  • Stress and tension
  • Lack of regular exercise
  • Poorly designed workstations
